    About Game

    Halo Infinite is a first-person shooter developed by 343 Industries and published by Xbox Game Studios. Released on December 8, 2021, the game marks the return of Master Chief in a new adventure within the Halo universe. Set on the semi-open world of Zeta Halo, Halo Infinite features exploration, tactical combat, and a story centered around the fight against the Banished, a powerful enemy faction that has seized control of the ring. The free-to-play multiplayer includes classic and new modes such as Big Team Battle and Fiesta, along with a seasonal system, armor customization, and live events.
